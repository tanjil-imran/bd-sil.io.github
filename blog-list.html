What is e-mail security & Why it is essential?

Email security refers to the methods and strategies used to secure email accounts and email contain’s information and communication from unwanted access, loss, or compromise. Because over 90% of all cyberattacks on businesses start with a

malicious email, relying on its built-in protection may leave your business vulnerable to cyber thieves who often exploit human error and lack of security. Because of cyber dangers such as social attacks that target enterprises via email, email protection is essential. Phishing emails, for example, may deceive users into divulging personal information, accepting false bills, or downloading malware that can infect your company's network. Threat actors rely on unsecured email systems to propagate malware and breach company IT security defenses because they provide them with various possibilities. Organizations must prioritize email security as part of their broader integrated IT security strategy. A successful phishing attack can cause significant downtime, irreversible reputation harm, and financial loss for businesses of all sizes.

Cyber-Security in Bangladesh?

criminal activities. If we examine the nature of cyber-crimes then we have a clear picture of cyber security condition in Bangladesh. When we start to think about cyber security in Bangladesh, the words stuck in our mind that are ‘pirated software’ and poor infrastructural system to protect cyber space in Bangladesh. In Bangladesh, around 90% of software is pirated. Using pirated software has become a culture and habit to Bangladesh people. This habit of using pirated software is leading us to more vulnerable position in the cyber security domain.
To understand the challenges of cyber security in Bangladesh, first we need to be aware of the nature of cyber-crimes in Bangladesh that we are facing day to day life. We can divide it into four categories. First, cyber-crimes that are targeting individuals, such as: hacking or cracking, illegal/unauthorized access, illegal interception, data interferences, E-mail spoofing, spamming, cheating and fraud, harassment and cyber stalking, defamation, drug trafficking, transmitting virus and worms, intellectual property crimes, computer and network resources vandalism, internet time and information thefts, forgery, denial of services, dissemination of obscene material etc. Second one is cyber-crime against property such as: credit card fund stealing, intellectual property crimes, internet time theft etc. Third one is crime against organizations. Such crime examples are like unauthorized control/access over the network resources and websites, exposing indecent/obscene materials over the web pages, virus attack, E-mail bombing, logic bombing, Trojan horse, data diddling, blocking from access, theft of important possessions, terrorism against government organizations, vandalizing the infrastructure of the network etc. Fourth and last category of cyber-crimes are happening against the society or social values of Bangladesh. Such crimes are like forgery, online gambling, trafficking, pornography (especially child pornography), financial crimes, polluting the youth through indecent exposure, web jacking etc.

What is digital marketing & benefits of it?

Digital marketing is promotion of brands advertising and selling products to consumers through the internet, mobile devices, social media, search engines and other forms of digital communication.

Digital Marketing:
Digital marketing is promotion of brands advertising and selling products to consumers through the internet, mobile devices, social media, search engines and other forms of digital communication.
With more than 80% of purchasers conducting product research online and over one billion people using search engines like Google each month, digital marketing is becoming increasingly vital for reaching customers.
#Benefits of Digital Marketing:
Businesses are taking use of the digital world's prospects now more than ever. To put it another way, online marketing has a significant impact on consumer purchasing decisions.
The overall goal of marketing is to engage with the target audience at the right place and at the right time, and what better location to interact with your audience than the internet?
1.Increase Customer Loyalty by Communicating Frequently:
It's nice to close a sale with your consumers, but keeping them thereafter is even better. A satisfied consumer is more likely to buy from you again or suggest your brand to others, resulting in more sales. Repeat consumers, who make up 8% of site visitors in the United States, account for 40% of online purchasing revenue. Keep in touch with customers to retain them, and digital marketing makes this easier than ever before.
2.Engage with Customer in their every Purchase:
Leading to the rise of digital marketing, few people discussed the customer journey because it was difficult to comprehend and analyze. A customer would often travel to a real business after viewing an advertisement on television or in the newspaper, make a decision, and pay for the product or service inside the store. In digital marketing, however, we can trace every step a customer takes, frequently starting from the time they are prompted to look for a solution or product.
Nowadays, it's normal for a consumer to view an advertisement online, search and compare costs on several websites, then visit a store to try the goods or shop online.
3.Find the Correct Audience:
Many digital marketing tools now allow you to target a specific group, making segmenting audiences easier than ever. We have access to web tools that track a person's online behaviors as well as demographic data. This information can be used by digital marketers to target consumers with specific products or services.
4.Measure Accurate Results:
It's difficult to track the success of your marketing activities when you're doing offline marketing. If you place an advertisement in a newspaper and on a billboard at the same time, you won't know how many people saw the newspaper ad or how many saw the billboard ad.
However, there are no such concerns with digital marketing. In fact, you can track everything worth tracking with digital marketing. From the moment the visitor clicks on the ad until they exit the page. Everything! You can track how many people saw your advertisement, how many purchases it generated, and, ultimately, your return on investment (ROI).
5.Create Brand Value:
The internet is the best way for any brand to establish trust rapidly and sustainably. According to content marketing studies, digitally active brands have the most credibility. A company's content can address customers' concerns, clarify key concepts, and demonstrate that it understands the market dynamics. By doing digital marketing everyone can know about your existence & what you do.
6.Cost-Effective For Every Organization:
Whether you're looking to advertise your company local or foreign, digital marketing offers cost-effective options. It enables even tiny businesses to compete with larger corporations by employing highly targeted techniques. To begin with, most of these tactics will be completely free (such as SEO, social media, and content marketing). However, not every type of digital marketing is appropriate for every company, and some may even be more expensive. Based on its marketing objectives, a company might locate relevant solutions.
